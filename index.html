The Challenge

Why Brands Haven't Entered Korea — Yet

These barriers are real. But they don't have to be your problem to solve. 😅

🏢
No Korean Entity
Setting up a Korean corporation takes months, legal fees, and a local director. Most brands don't have the bandwidth.
🛃
Import Complexity
Korean customs, food labeling laws, and import clearance are highly specific. One wrong label = shipment rejected.
🗣️
No Local Team
Korean customer service, platform management, and logistics all require people on the ground — speaking Korean.
💸
High Setup Cost
Hiring a local employee costs $60K+ per year before benefits. That's before you've sold a single unit.
